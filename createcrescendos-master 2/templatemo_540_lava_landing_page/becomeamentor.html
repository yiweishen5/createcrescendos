Why Become a Mentor?

01

Impact

By being a mentor, you will have opportunities to directly help younger music students improve, possbily even creating a lasting impact on their lives. You will have the power to create lasting, fulfilling connections.

02

Flexibility

The times are completely up to you to decide! Once a prospective student chooses to connect with you, you will contact them through email or text to schedule the session. The date, time, and details of the session are flexible.

03

Improvement

Not only can you improve your own musical skills by teaching others, but you can also improve your resume through CreateCrescendos' opportunities to give back to your community and beyond.
